Prizes

A little taster of the glorious riches up for grabs... Click the prize name to learn more about the amazing women behind the awards.

🏆 Winner

Prize: Two tickets of their choice to visit Buckingham Palace, Saint Paul’s, Tower of London, Windsor Castle or Stonehenge

🧪 Marie Curie Runner-Up Prize

Prize: One ticket of their choice to visit Buckingham Palace, Saint Paul’s, Tower of London, Windsor Castle or Stonehenge

📐 Katherine Johnson Prize for Most Kills

Prize: One ticket of their choice to visit Buckingham Palace, Saint Paul’s, Tower of London, Windsor Castle or Stonehenge

If two or more players tie, the prize goes to the player who reached that total first.

🎬 Hedy Lamarr Prize for Best Kill

Prize: One ticket of their choice to visit Buckingham Palace, Saint Paul’s or the Tower of London

Awarded by the umpires based on historical accuracy, costumes, choice of weapon, and description.

💻 Ada Lovelace Prize for Most Kills in a Day

Prize: One ticket of their choice to visit Buckingham Palace, Saint Paul’s or the Tower of London

Tie-break rules:
– Winner is whoever achieved that number of kills in a day most often.
– If still tied, shortest time between first and last kill on that day wins.

Huge thanks to Handshake for sponsoring our first prize, and WSET (Women in STEM society) for supporting the remaining prizes!
