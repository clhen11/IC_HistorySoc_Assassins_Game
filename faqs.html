FAQs
How can I find my target?
Be creative and use your tools! You can try Celcat, LinkedIn, Instagram and any other tool you can think of to find a time and place where you can get to your Target!
What if I can’t find my target?
After a week of trying to find your target, if they have an Inactive status then you can request to Skip them (see Skipping above).
What if I want to leave the game?
If you decide you no longer want to be part of the game, just email the umpires at icassassinsgame@gmail.com and we’ll remove you.
I have a problem/question/suggestion regarding the Game, who should I reach out to?
Please email icassassinsgame@gmail.com, or contact an umpire directly: Vittorio (vs1022@ic.ac.uk), Will (wx1424@ic.ac.uk), or Cazza (csc23@ic.ac.uk).
I am a committee member from another society and we would like our events to be Out of Bounds. What should I do?
Please let the umpires know at icassassinsgame@gmail.com and we’ll update the rules and OoB list accordingly.
